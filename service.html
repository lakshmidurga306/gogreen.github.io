🌿 Our Services


At Go Green, we provide the following eco-friendly solutions to support your green journey:


🌱 Home Gardening Guidance

We help you grow your own food in your backyard or balcony! Here's how:

  1. Site Selection: We guide you to choose the right location with enough sunlight.
  2. Soil Preparation: Learn to enrich soil using organic matter and compost.
  3. Plant Selection: We recommend beginner-friendly vegetables and herbs like tomatoes, mint, and coriander.
  4. Watering Tips: We advise on when and how much to water your plants for healthy growth.

♻️ Compost Making Assistance

We teach you how to turn kitchen waste into powerful organic compost:

  • ✅ Collect vegetable peels, fruit scraps, and used tea leaves.
  • ✅ Mix with dry leaves or newspaper for carbon balance.
  • ✅ Use compost bins or natural pits — we help you set it up.
  • ✅ Within 45–60 days, get ready-to-use compost for your plants.

🌾 Natural Fertilizers for Healthy Crops

We recommend and provide guidance on using eco-friendly fertilizers such as:

  • 🔸 Vermicompost (worm compost)
  • 🔸 Cow dung manure
  • 🔸 Banana peel compost
  • 🔸 Eggshell powder for calcium boost
  • 🔸 Neem cake powder to improve soil and prevent pests
